基礎(ひらがな/カタカナ)
--> A dictionary for Beginners who had no basic about ひらがな and カタカナ
数字(Number)
--> It is the most important part tolearn and know to speak number in japanese
年,月,曜日(Year, Month, Day)
--> You should know year,month,day in japanese to use it to dialog fluently.
文法(Grammer)
--> You should know basic grammer in japanese to use it to dialog fluently.
당신의 레벨은 어느정도 입니까? / あなたのレベルはどのくらいですか?
初心者(Easy)
--> After master ひらがな and カタカナ, then start it with basic level between N5~N4
中級者(Normal)
--> People who had basic about ひらがな and カタカナ and leearn some vocabulary
上級者(Hard)
--> After finished Normal level you can challenge Hard mode that who can understand N2 level vocabulary
マスター(Expert)
--> If you master most of vocabulary, you can try Master level and proof your level!
